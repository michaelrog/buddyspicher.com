Those Swingin' Spicher Boys ... Matt, Buddy and David, at The Bluewind

The Nashville Swing Band plays Wednesday nights at The Bluewind, in The Factory in Franklin, TN, just 15 minutes south of Nashville.  The Factory is a shopping, dining and entertainment complex in the old Magic Chef oven factory.  The Bluewind features live music and great food in an eclectic, casual atmosphere.  It's a great place to catch Buddy's band and some of Nashville's finest musicians who make guest appearances.  The Band plays from 7:30 p.m. until 11:00 p.m.

Directions to The Bluewind:  South from Nashville on I-65 toward Franklin, take the Cool Springs exit, west to Mack Hatcher Blvd.  Turn left, and go to the next traffic light which is Liberty Pike.  Go right on Liberty Pike until you reach Franklin Rd., and you're there.
