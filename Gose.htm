Gose Guitars

Georg Gose is a Master Luthier whose workshop and showroom is in the town of Vallendar, just across the Rhein River from Koblenz, Germany.  His specialty is "plucked" acoustic instruments, and he crafts lutes, classical guitars and jazz guitars from the finest air-dried woods.  

His web site can be found here.

The jazz guitar shown below is made from woods which have been naturally dried for more than thirty years.  The top is Black Forest Alpine Spruce, back and sides are flamed European maple, bound with Rosewood.  The neck is Spanish Cedar, the fingerboard is Ebony.  The tuners are hand made in Germany with Ebony knobs.
