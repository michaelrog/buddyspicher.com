Josh's CD is a really lively collection of twelve Western Swing Favorites.  Buddy produced the album and plays twin fiddles with Josh on several of the songs, including Faded Love and Maiden's Prayer.  Kurt Storey joins them on Lily Dale, for some beautiful three-fiddle harmony.   The entire CD was recorded, mixed and mastered by Kurt at Buddy's Studio, The Fiddle House.
