Josh Hedley ....  "A Tribute to Bob Wills"

Guess how hard it would be to convince Buddy to produce an album of Western Swing Music?  (Of course, he loves it.)   And this time, one of his students was the artist.  Buddy had met Josh at Mark O'Connor's Fiddle Camp several years prior to the June, 2000 Camp.  Josh had worked long and hard at improving both his playing ability and his delivery as an entertainer.  He was determined to become a polished entertainer, and this year he felt he was ready to record his CD.  Josh had been playing in the Ft. Meyers, Florida area, so he was "in voice" and ready .... 

  Josh Hedley    "A Tribute To Bob Wills"

Of course, Josh wanted the recordings to be the best they could be, so he arranged for Buddy to bring in some of the best Western Swing musicians.  Not too difficult a task, considering that Buddy's sons David and Matt are tops on Acoustic Bass and Swing Guitar.  Add to that Jim White, Crystal Gayle's drummer, and Jody Nardone on Piano and Tommy Hannum on Steel Guitar, well.... now you have a real swing band to provide the foundations for this 15-year old fiddler / vocalist.  And oh yes, Buddy knows how to play second fiddle ... just a little bit.   

Was it good?  Well, at the Showboat Hotel and Casino in  LasVegas where the Country Music Organizations of America  was held in October, Josh's album was awarded the CD the "Album, Cassette, CD of the Year Award" at the American Eagle Awards in the 13-16 age group.

Josh also took Youth Instrumentalist, with "Faded Love" and "Orange Blossom Special", Youth Entertainer - New Country (South's Gonna Do it Again and Devil Went Down to Georgia") and Youth Male Vocalist Traditional with songs from his CD, "Roly Poly"  and "Right or Wrong",  and Youth Male Vocalist Traditional Entertainer with two more songs from his CD, "Miss Molly" and "Take Me Back to Tulsa".  We'd say the album and Josh did well. 

And this is great publicity for Buddy's producing talent, and for The Fiddle House, and certainly for the very talented young performer and fiddler, Josh Hedley. More information on Josh's CD can be found by clicking here, and its available for purchase from SecTer Records.
