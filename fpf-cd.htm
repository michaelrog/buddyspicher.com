Buddy plays "old time"  fiddle favorites in a collection called "Front Porch Fiddle"

Buddy created this CD in the true old-time fiddle style.   With Buddy's son, David, on Bass and Shad Cobb on banjo, mandolin and guitar, Buddy has captured the great  fiddle band sound, true to it American roots.  Selections include:
