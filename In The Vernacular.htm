An absolutely uncompromising and outstanding selection of classic fiddle tunes performed by the "Master of Double Stops" himself.    Clean, bright, precise, beautifully recorded; a true listening pleasure, time after time.

Featured on this collection is Buddy on violin and viola, with his favorite twin fiddle partner, the incredible Billy Contreras.  Buddy's son David is on Acoustic Bass;  long time Swing Band favorite Paul Kramer appears on guitar and mandolin; and Shad Cobb appears on mandolin, banjo, violin and guitar. 

15 Selections include:

Lonesome Indian
Twinkle Twinkle Little Star (Western Swing version)
St. Ann's Reel
Beautiful Dreamer
Brilliance
Have You Ever See the Devil, Uncle Joe?
I Dream of Jeanie with the Light Brown Hair
Whistlin' Rufus
Bear Creek Hop
Lonesome Indian  II
Up on Cripple Creek
Down in the Valley
Rubber Dolly
Bully of the Town
Sally Goodin'

 "In The Vernacular"
