Nashville Fiddler  "Portrait"

 

An exciting selection of tunes performed in Buddy's trademark double stops, including  great Texas Swing, Buddy's originals, medleys of familiar songs, and a wonderful traditional version of  "Cotton Eyed Joe".   

" Portrait"       Seventeen Songs & Tunes

Bob Wills Medley - (The Gal I Left Behind, Chinese Breakdown, Liberty, Redwing, and The Lone Star Rag)
Little Liz Jane - (Buddy sings this one!)
Uncle Pen - ( featuring Michael Kott)
Little Betty Brown (thanks to Randy Elmore for know the name of this tune!)
Stephen Foster Medley - (Oh Susannah, Camptown Races, The Old Folks at Home, Buffalo Gals, Old Black Joe, My Old Kentucky Home)
Ashokan Farewell
Sally Johnson
Spanish Two Step
Last Date
Black & White Rag
In the Shadow of the Pines (Michael Kott on vocal)
Buddy's Swing (or, Buddy's Raised Fifth)
Silver Bell
Cotton Eyed Joe  (featuring Buddy with both great Texas Fiddlers Terry and Dale Morris from 1982!  Thank you Dale!)
Samson's Fury
Paula's Waltz   (Buddy's original tune, now a contest standard)
Buddy's Sally Johnson  (a little swing added, just for the fun of it - don't try this at home)

                                       

   This  CD was produced and recorded at Buddy's Studio, The Fiddle House.
