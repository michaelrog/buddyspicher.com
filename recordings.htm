Three new CDs for 2001

...featuring Buddy's great fiddle and arrangements:  a new collection of fiddle tunes & favorites, called "Rags to Waltzes".   And there's two new Nashville Swing Band CDs, one filled with  great standards, titled, "Swing Time", and a new NSB "Western Swing" CD, titled "Western Swing" (what else?) !

The new Nashville Swing Band swing CDs  feature the incomparable jazz pianist,  Beegie Adair  on the Yamaha C7.   She often joins the Swing Band at The Bluewind Jazz Cafe in Franklin, TN on Thursday evenings.  

Out of retirement just for his friend Buddy Spicher, the man who invented the Pedal Steel Guitar,  the great Buddy Emmons adds his touch to the swing CDs.   Featured vocalists include Pam Tillis, Crystal Gayle, Andrea Ladd, Matt and David Spicher, and Robin Contreras.   

And for something VERY special, Leon Rausch, the voice of The Texas Playboys, sings a dozen songs on the two CDs.   We saw Leon performing with the Playboys at Michael Martin Murphy's "Westfest" in Colorado where Buddy was appearing  with Crystal Gayle. 

Here's s a snapshot of David Spicher, with Leon and Buddy, just after finishing up the sessions.   

Both David and Matt Spicher shared  the engineering tasks on the recording sessions which Buddy produced.   Nashville Swing Band members appearing include Beegie Adair on piano, David on upright bass, Jim White on drums, and Billy Contreras on fiddle.
