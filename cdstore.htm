We have added this feature to assist you in purchasing the CDs featured on Buddy's web site.  It uses a service called "Paypal", and allows you to safely and securely send and receive payments over the internet.  It also allows you to use your Visa or MasterCard.

Of course, you can still use the mail, and send payment by personal check or money order, payable to "SecTer Records".  The address is:
