Buddy plays fiddle favorites including rags, waltzes, reels, breakdowns, hoedowns, goosedowns, polkas, and jigs, (but no marches, tangos, sambas, or cha-cha's...).  

Helping him out on twin fiddles is Billy Contreras, and David Spicher is on bass.  Guitarists include Ranger Doug Green, from "Riders in the Sky", and Andy Most.  Leven Roman adds a little flute, and Duane Norman plays drums on a few cuts.
